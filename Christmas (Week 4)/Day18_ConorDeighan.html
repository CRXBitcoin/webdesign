Why did the Christmas ornament go to therapy?
Because it couldn't handle being hung up every year!

Day 18

Christmas ornaments are festive decorations hung on holiday trees to add color,
sparkle, and personality to the season. They come in many forms—shiny glass balls,
glittering snowflakes, wooden stars, tiny figurines, and more—each bringing its
own charm and warmth. Whether elegant, rustic, or playful, ornaments help create
a cozy, magical atmosphere and make the Christmas tree a joyful centerpiece of
holiday celebrations.
