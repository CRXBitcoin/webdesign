What We're Raising Money For

At Children in Need, our mission is simple: to ensure every child across the UK has the chance to thrive. The money you help us raise supports children and young people facing poverty, disability, illness, trauma, or other disadvantages. We fund local projects, youth groups, mental-health programmes, crisis support, and community organisations that work directly with the children who need us most. Every donation—big or small—helps us remove barriers and brighten futures.
